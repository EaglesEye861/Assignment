BCA(BACHELOR OF COMPUTER APPLICATION)



PROGRAMME INFORMATION

BCA is a three-year undergraduate degree course in the field of computer applications. It is a program designed for students who want to learn computer languages and build their career as programmer or developer in the IT industry. This course helps the students in developing programming skills, networking skills, programming languages and application packages. BCA is equivalent to a B.E or B.Tech in Computer Science.

ELIGIBILITY

The candidate should have passed 10+2 exam from any recognized Board/Council. The candidate must have Mathematics/Statistics/BM/Computer Science as one of the subjects at the 10+2 level.
