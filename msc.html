MSC IN COMPUTER SCIENCE










PROGRAMME INFORMATION

M.Sc(Computer Science)helps you to gain a scientific and practical approach to computation. This course is designed to provide a thorough knowledge and practical experience in the field of computer systems and information technology such as programming, software engineering, database management etc. With the help of dedicated faculties, students get an insight about the complex computer languages and applications. A professional degree in Computer Science prepares the students in building a solid foundation in science and mathematical principles and opens up a host of careers in the IT sector.

ELIGIBILITY

Three year Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) or Three Year Honours graduate (Computer Science/appropriate disciplines) or Engineering graduates (Mathematics/Physics/appropriate discipline).
